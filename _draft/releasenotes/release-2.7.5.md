# SpellActivationOverlay 2.7.5
@here I am pleased to announce the newest release of SpellActivationOverlay, version 2.7.5
## Classes
### :shaman:  Shaman
Shamans once again have pretty visuals for Elemental Focus (Classic Era, The Burning Crusade, Wrath of the Lich King).

These visuals are optional, and the pretty one is suggested by default, to match visuals prior to version 2.7.2.
## Bug Fixes
### Glowing Buttons
New in-game glowing buttons fixes have been added (Mists of Pandaria). For the record, these options shall go away when Blizzard fixes their game client.

These are the new temporary glowing buttons
- :paladin:  Paladin
  - Flash of Light, while Selfless Healer is at 3 stacks
- :priest:  Priest
  - Shadow Word: Death
  - Power Word: Shield, during Divine Insight
  - Prayer of Mending, during Divine Insight
  - Mind Blast, during Divine Insight
## Known Limitations
Paladins have too many glowing button options (Mists of Pandaria).
